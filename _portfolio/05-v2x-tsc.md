**Role**: Research Developer
**Period**: Sept 2021 – Present
**Funding**: US-DOT, Michigan DOT, Leidos Inc.

## Part 1: Smart Intersection Project
- Implemented CV2X system for Transit Signal Priority (TSP) in City of Ann Arbor.
- Redeveloped Multi-Modal Intelligent Traffic Signal System (MMITSS). [GitHub](https://github.com/Dylan-Wyl10/mmitss-CART)
- Maintained and updated versions on [road-side](https://hub.docker.com/r/cartlabpurdue/mmitss-mrp-x86) and [vehicle-side](https://hub.docker.com/r/cartlabpurdue/mmitss-vsp-x86) applications.
- Conducted field testing, installation, and data-processing for buses and real intersections. [GitHub](https://github.com/Dylan-Wyl10/SIP-MMITSS)

## Part 2: Michigan-DOT ATCMTD Project
- Implementing MMITSS system in [Q-Line](https://www.qlinedetroit.com/) in Detroit, MI.
- Updated MMITSS to accommodate NTCIP 1211 operation protocol. [GitHub](https://github.com/Dylan-Wyl10/mmitss-CART/tree/Dev-1211)

## Part 3: CDA-sim — Integrating MMITSS on a Co-simulation Platform
- Integrating MMITSS into CARMA-CARLA-SUMO co-simulation.
- Developed time-synchronization, message brokers, and parallel processing to support MMITSS in the CDA-Sim platform. [GitHub](https://github.com/usdot-fhwa-stol/cda-mmitss/tree/develop)
- Built integration testing and demo with released configuration.
