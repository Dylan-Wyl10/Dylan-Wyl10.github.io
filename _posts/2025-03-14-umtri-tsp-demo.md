Our CART Lab at Purdue University demonstrated a live Transit Signal Priority (TSP) system at the [University of Michigan Transportation Research Institute (UMTRI) 60th Anniversary Symposium](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/purduecce_cart-activity-7391571672681672705-yhY4) in Ann Arbor, MI.

## The Demo

A transit bus equipped with V2X onboard units drove through Mcity, showcasing how TSP leverages Vehicle-to-Everything (V2X) communication to enable smarter, more adaptive traffic signals — improving mobility and efficiency for priority vehicles in real-world traffic.

## Real-World Deployment

This same system is already operational along the Ellsworth corridor in southern Ann Arbor, serving Route 6 buses since August 2024, as part of the U.S. Department of Transportation's **Smart Intersections** project.
